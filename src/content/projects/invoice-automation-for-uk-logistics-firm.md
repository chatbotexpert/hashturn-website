## The Challenge

George's logistics company was processing dozens of orders per day, each requiring a manually created invoice. The team was spending 3+ hours every day copy-pasting data from order forms into invoice templates, generating PDFs, emailing customers, and updating their CRM — a repetitive process prone to errors and delays.

## Our Solution

We built a fully automated invoicing pipeline using **Make.com** as the orchestration layer:

1. **Order Trigger** — When a new order is logged in Google Sheets, the automation fires instantly
2. **Data Extraction** — Key fields (customer name, items, quantities, pricing) are pulled and validated
3. **PDF Generation** — A professional invoice PDF is generated from a branded template
4. **Email Delivery** — The invoice is automatically emailed to the customer via Gmail with the correct subject line and body copy
5. **CRM Update** — The customer record in their CRM is updated with invoice status and payment due date

## Results

- **3 hours/day saved** — staff reallocated to higher-value work
- **Zero manual errors** — consistent, accurate invoices every time
- **Faster payments** — invoices sent within seconds of order, not hours
- **Full audit trail** — every invoice logged with timestamp and delivery status
