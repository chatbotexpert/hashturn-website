## The Challenge

A mid-size company in the UAE had no formal document approval process. Contracts, purchase orders, and policy documents were being shared over email chains, making it impossible to track who approved what, and when. The IT department was spending 15+ hours per week manually chasing approvers and tracking document status.

## Our Solution

We designed and built a **multi-stage approval workflow** entirely within Microsoft 365 — no new tools or subscriptions required:

1. **Document Submission** — Staff upload documents to a designated SharePoint library using a standardised naming convention
2. **Stage 1 Review** — The direct line manager receives a Teams notification with an embedded Approve/Reject button
3. **Stage 2 Review** — On approval, the document escalates to the department head for final sign-off
4. **Deadline Reminders** — Automated reminders sent at 24h and 48h if no action is taken
5. **Audit Trail** — All decisions, timestamps, and comments are logged in a SharePoint list for compliance

## Tools Used

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Power Automate | Workflow logic and routing |
| SharePoint | Document storage and tracking list |
| Microsoft Teams | Approval notifications and actions |
| Outlook | Email fallback for external approvers |

## Results

- **15+ hours/week saved** for the IT department
- **100% traceability** — every decision logged with user, timestamp, and comments
- **Faster decisions** — average approval time reduced from 4 days to under 6 hours
- **Compliance ready** — full audit trail meets internal governance requirements
